Digital Declaration has been created to help law firms and the clients they represent.
Right now there's a gap in knowledge for many conmpanies and firms in relation to the
when and how Electronic data and law intersect with each other. In order to change this, the gap in knowledge needs to be bridged for both your clients
and your firm. However most firms don't have in-house staff who are technically adept
on data management and many companies of all sizes don't know what could or will be
required should any of their data become evidence. We're here to help take you and your
clients from a reactive state to a pro-active state, to give you with more time in defense
prep and less time fact finding.

This site also contains several resource pages to help you and your staff understand terms
like ESI, TAR, newsfeeds, and a page to help you assess how ready you are to present a
clients data in court.
